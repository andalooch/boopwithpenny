The story behind boop

Penny moved to a new town and didn't know anyone.

So her dad built her an app. Boop was created by a dog dad who wanted his mini goldendoodle to make friends in the neighborhood. Built from scratch, just for Penny — and now for every dog who deserves a best friend. Now Penny has plenty. And yours can too.

Features

Everything your dog needs
to make a friend

🐾
Swipe on dogs, not people
Dog photos front and centre. Because this app is about your dog, not a dating profile.
🛡️
Safety filters
Choose to only see women owners, couples, or no preference. You're always in control of who sees you.
📅
Built-in meetup scheduler
Propose a time, place and get confirmation — all without leaving the app. No more "let's figure it out" messages.
🟢
Live park status
Heading to the dog park? Let nearby owners know. See who's there right now and spontaneously meet up.
🧠
Dog personality quiz
A 5-question quiz generates your dog's personality type and shows compatibility scores with nearby dogs.
👥
Swipe with a friend
Group swipe mode lets two owners swipe together. When you both boop someone, it's a group match!

How it works

Three steps to your dog's new best friend

1
Sniff
Browse nearby dogs sorted by distance. Set your safety filters and swipe on the ones that look like a good match.
2
Boop
When both owners boop each other, it's a match. You'll get a notification and can start chatting immediately.
3
Meet
Use the built-in meetup scheduler to propose a time and place. Get confirmation and do the safety check-in when you arrive.
